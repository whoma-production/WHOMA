## Who can sign up?
WHOMA is currently focused on estate agents and estate-agency professionals. Public signup is designed for agents.

Homeowner access is limited during the early access period and may be routed through support or controlled pilot flows.

## How do agents create an account?
Agents can create an account from the WHOMA sign-up page using the authentication methods currently enabled for the live service.

After account creation, agents are guided through role selection and onboarding before they can complete their public profile.

## What email should agents use?
Agents should use an email address they can access reliably. WHOMA may ask agents to verify a work email during onboarding so the profile can be reviewed with stronger trust signals.

## I did not receive a confirmation email
Ask the user to check spam or junk folders, confirm the email address was entered correctly, and wait a few minutes in case of provider delays.

If the email still does not arrive, tell them a real person can help and that they can click "Talk to a person" in the chat.

## Can homeowners sign up?
WHOMA's public self-serve signup is currently agent-first. Homeowners can still use public pages to learn about WHOMA and may be invited into controlled collaboration workflows as the platform expands.
