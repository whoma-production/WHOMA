## What is WHOMA?
WHOMA is a UK platform for independent estate agents to build verified professional profiles and for homeowners to find agents with clearer proof of experience.

WHOMA stands for "Where Home Owners Meet Agents". The current product focus is verified estate-agent identity, profile quality, past deal evidence, and a controlled homeowner collaboration pilot.

## Who is WHOMA for?
WHOMA is primarily for independent estate agents and estate-agency professionals who want to show credible proof of their work beyond their agency brand.

Homeowner access is currently more limited. Homeowners can browse public agent profiles and selected public request surfaces as WHOMA develops the wider instruction and proposal workflow.

## What problem does WHOMA solve?
Agents often rely on agency-level reputation, even when their personal track record is what homeowners care about. WHOMA helps agents collect structured professional information, publish a profile, and add verifiable evidence such as past deals.

## How does WHOMA work at a high level?
Agents create an account, complete onboarding, verify their work email, build a structured profile, add past deals where available, and submit their profile for review.

Published and verified profiles can appear in the public agent directory. Future marketplace flows connect homeowner instructions with structured agent proposals.

## Is WHOMA a property portal?
WHOMA is not a traditional property portal. It is focused on estate-agent identity, trust, verification, and structured homeowner-agent collaboration.
