## What does WHOMA verify?
WHOMA can verify parts of an agent's profile and evidence, including work email access, profile readiness, and past deal confirmations where available.

## What is work email verification?
Work email verification confirms that an agent can access the email address associated with their professional profile.

## What is admin review?
Admin review is WHOMA's internal review process for deciding whether an agent profile should be marked as verified or remain pending.

## What does pending mean?
Pending means the profile or evidence is waiting for review, confirmation, or additional information.

## What does disputed mean?
Disputed means a seller or reviewer has challenged a past deal or evidence item. Disputed evidence should not be presented as verified.

## Is verification a recommendation?
No. Verification is a trust signal about identity or evidence. It is not a recommendation, legal advice, financial advice, or a guarantee of results.

## Why did verification change after profile edits?
If an agent materially changes profile claims after verification, WHOMA may return the profile to pending review so the new information can be checked.
