## What happens during agent onboarding?
Agent onboarding collects the core information needed to create a credible WHOMA profile. This can include name, agency, job title, contact details, service areas, specialties, professional bio, and working style.

## Can agents upload a CV or resume?
WHOMA supports an AI-assisted profile generation flow where agents can upload a CV or resume or provide profile text. Suggestions are review-first and should be checked before publishing.

## Does WHOMA auto-publish imported information?
No. Imported or AI-suggested information should be treated as a draft. Agents are expected to review, confirm, edit, and publish their own profile information.

## Why does onboarding ask for service areas and specialties?
Service areas and specialties help homeowners understand where an agent works and what kinds of property or transaction work they focus on.

## Why is work email verification part of onboarding?
Work email verification helps WHOMA confirm that the agent can access the professional email address linked to their profile. It is one trust signal used before stronger public visibility.

## What if onboarding gets stuck?
Ask the user to refresh, sign back in, and retry the step. If the issue persists, tell them a real person can help and that they can click "Talk to a person" in the chat.
