## What is a WHOMA agent profile?
A WHOMA agent profile is a structured professional page for an estate agent. It can include biography, years of experience, service areas, specialties, achievements, languages, working style, and trust signals.

## What makes a profile publish-ready?
A profile should include enough accurate professional information for homeowners to understand who the agent is, where they work, and what they can help with.

WHOMA may require profile completeness and work email verification before publishing.

## Who can see public profiles?
Public agent profiles are intended for homeowners and other visitors who want to assess an agent's professional credibility.

Only profiles that meet WHOMA's visibility rules should appear publicly.

## What does verified mean?
Verified means WHOMA has reviewed enough profile or evidence information to mark the agent as verified. Verification is not a guarantee of future performance and should not be treated as legal, financial, or property advice.

## Can agents edit their profile?
Agents can edit their profile information. Material changes to verified claims may require further review before the profile is treated as verified again.

## Why does profile quality matter?
Profile quality helps homeowners compare agents more fairly. A complete profile gives clearer context than a name, agency, and phone number alone.
